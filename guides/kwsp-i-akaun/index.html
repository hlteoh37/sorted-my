💰 EPF i-Akaun Complete Guide

Register, check your KWSP balance, make withdrawals, and update your details — all in one place.

What is i-Akaun? i-Akaun is EPF's (KWSP) official member portal. It lets you check your balance, view contribution history, manage nominations, and apply for withdrawals — without visiting an EPF branch.

How to Register for EPF i-Akaun

  1. Go to i.kwsp.gov.my or download the i-Akaun app
    The portal and app are both official EPF platforms. The mobile app (i-Akaun) is available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. Search "i-Akaun" — the developer should be "Kumpulan Wang Simpanan Pekerja."
  2. Click "Register" and enter your IC number
    You'll be asked for your MyKad (IC) number and date of birth. Your username for i-Akaun is always your IC number.
  3. Verify via OTP to your EPF-registered mobile number
    EPF will send a one-time password (OTP) to the mobile number registered with EPF. If you have never updated your mobile number with EPF, or if the number has changed, you must visit an EPF branch first to update it before you can register online.
  4. Set your password
    Create a password that meets EPF's requirements (minimum 8 characters, mix of letters, numbers, and symbols). Do not share this password.
  5. Log in and complete any additional verification
    First-time logins may require you to confirm your email address or complete a security question setup. Once done, you have full access to your i-Akaun.
Mobile number not registered with EPF? You must visit any EPF branch with your MyKad to register or update your mobile number before you can use i-Akaun. You cannot register online if EPF has no mobile number on record for you.

What You Can Do in i-Akaun

Check Your Balance

Log in → Dashboard. You'll see your three account balances at a glance:

AccountPurposeAccess
Akaun Persaraan (Akaun 1)Retirement savings — 75% of contributionsAt retirement age (55)
Akaun Sejahtera (Akaun 2)Housing, education, health — 15% of contributionsSpecific withdrawals
Akaun Fleksibel (Akaun 3)Flexible cash — 10% of contributionsAnytime (min RM50)

View Contribution History

Go to Contributions → Statement. You can view monthly contributions by your employer and yourself, check if contributions are being paid correctly, and download PDF statements for bank loan applications.

Update Personal Details

  • Mobile number — update via i-Akaun or EPF branch
  • Email address — update under Profile settings
  • Bank account number — required for withdrawals; must be in your own name
  • Address — update under Profile (does not update your MyKad address)

Manage Nominations

Go to Nomination → View/Update. Your EPF nomination determines who receives your savings if you die before retirement. This is separate from your will — EPF pays nominees directly, bypassing the estate distribution process. Update it after marriage, divorce, or having children.

Apply for Withdrawals

Available withdrawal types via i-Akaun:

  • Akaun Fleksibel withdrawal — minimum RM50, once per month, credited within 1–3 working days
  • Housing withdrawal (Akaun Sejahtera) — to purchase or build a home, reduce housing loan, or pay monthly instalments
  • Education withdrawal (Akaun Sejahtera) — for approved tertiary institutions in Malaysia
  • Medical withdrawal (Akaun Sejahtera) — for critical illness treatment for member or dependants
  • Age 50 withdrawal — partial withdrawal allowed at age 50
  • Age 55 withdrawal (full) — full retirement withdrawal at age 55

Checking Your EPF Contributions Are Correct

Every month, check that your employer is contributing correctly. Log in to i-Akaun → Contributions → Monthly Statement. The statutory contribution rates are:

Employer not contributing? If your employer is missing or underpaying EPF contributions, you can file a complaint with EPF directly via i-Akaun (under Feedback/Complaint) or visit an EPF branch. EPF has enforcement powers to compel employers to pay.

Troubleshooting i-Akaun Problems

  • Can't receive OTP — your mobile number registered with EPF may be different from your current number. Visit any EPF branch with your MyKad to update it.
  • Account locked — too many wrong password attempts locks your account. Wait 24 hours or call EPF at 03-8922 6000 to unlock.
  • Forgot password — use the Forgot Password link on i.kwsp.gov.my. OTP is sent to your registered mobile. If you've lost access to that number, visit an EPF branch.
  • Contributions not showing — employer contributions are uploaded monthly. Allow up to 45 days after the contribution month before escalating.
  • Wrong name or IC number in EPF records — visit an EPF branch with your MyKad and birth certificate to correct it. Cannot be done online.

Pro Tips

  • Check your EPF statement every quarter — verify contributions are being paid and the amounts are correct. Errors discovered late are harder to recover.
  • Update your nomination after every major life event — EPF nomination bypasses your will. After marriage or divorce, update immediately or your savings may go to the wrong person.
  • Download your EPF statement for home loan applications — banks require 3–6 months of EPF statements as proof of income. i-Akaun generates these instantly as a PDF.
  • Enable i-Akaun notifications — the app can send alerts when contributions are received. Useful for detecting missed employer contributions early.
  • Don't withdraw Akaun Fleksibel unnecessarily — every withdrawal reduces your long-term retirement savings. EPF's dividend averages 5–6% per year. Think of it as a 5% guaranteed return you're forgoing every time you withdraw early.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for EPF i-Akaun?
Go to i.kwsp.gov.my or download the i-Akaun mobile app. Click Register, enter your IC number, and verify via OTP sent to your EPF-registered mobile number. If your number isn't registered with EPF, visit any EPF branch to update it first.
What can I do on EPF i-Akaun?
i-Akaun lets you check your EPF balance across all three accounts, view contribution history, update personal details, manage nominations, apply for eligible withdrawals (housing, education, Akaun Fleksibel), and generate statements for loan or tax purposes.
I forgot my i-Akaun password. How do I reset it?
Go to i.kwsp.gov.my and click 'Forgot Password'. Enter your IC number and the OTP will be sent to your registered mobile. If you no longer have access to the registered mobile number, you must visit an EPF branch in person with your MyKad to reset your account.
How do I update my bank account in EPF i-Akaun?
Log in to i-Akaun, go to Profile > Bank Account Update. Enter your new bank account details. EPF will verify the account before activating it for withdrawals. The account must be in your own name — EPF does not transfer to third-party accounts.
Can I check my EPF balance without i-Akaun?
Yes. You can check your EPF balance at EPF kiosks (at branches and some banks), via SMS, or by visiting an EPF branch. However, i-Akaun provides the most detailed view including full contribution history, all three account balances, and nomination details.
How do I withdraw from Akaun Fleksibel via i-Akaun?
Log in to i-Akaun → Withdrawals → Akaun Fleksibel. Enter the amount (minimum RM50) and confirm your registered bank account. Approved withdrawals are credited within 1–3 working days. You can only make one withdrawal per month.
